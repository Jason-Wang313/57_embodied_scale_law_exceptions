# Novelty Boundary Map

## Not Novel

- Scaling laws.
- Robot foundation models.
- Contact-rich manipulation.
- Latency-aware control.
- Safety constraints.
- Morphology transfer.
- Sim-to-real transfer.

## Plausible Contribution

The contribution is the regime-conditioned reporting discipline and deterministic full-scale benchmark: evaluate scaling curves by physical regime and decompose errors into representation, contact, latency, safety, morphology, saturation, and intervention recovery.

## Final Boundary

The paper supports an empirical benchmark claim inside the deterministic suite. It does not claim universal exponents or hardware certification. V2 keyword sensitivity is retained as a negative control.
